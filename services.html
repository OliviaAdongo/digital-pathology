Mini Documentary:
The Pathology Network: Supporting surgical training in Chogoria Mission Hospital

As creative director and producer of this mini documentary commissioned by The Pathology Network, my work was to conceptualize and execute a short film, based on client prompts, telling the story of the relationship between The Pathology Network and PCEA Chogoria Hospital, emphasizing how access to good quality pathology diagnostic services is enhancing the training of surgeons.
