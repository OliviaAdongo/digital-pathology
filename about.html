About

Hi, I'm Maureen Waithaka

Why Digital Pathology?

I am a passionate digital pathologist, dedicated to revolutionizing diagnostics through innovation and technology. My mission is to leverage digital pathology to enhance accuracy, accessibility, and efficiency of surgical pathology diagnosis, ensuring better patient outcomes. I am also deeply committed to medical education, through which we can empower the next generation of pathologists and healthcare professionals.

How I Got Here

My journey in medicine began as a medical intern at a hospital in Meru County, Kenya. The work was profoundly meaningful, particularly seeing patients from distant areas receive good quality care at our facility. Unfortunately, this experience also revealed challenges in our healthcare system. Many patients, especially those in underserved communities, have limited access to surgical pathology diagnoses. This type of diagnosis is critical for effective cancer management. We simply don't have enough pathologists to serve our growing populations. Digital pathology offers a powerful solution to bridge these crucial gaps in care.

My Values & Beliefs

Innovation & Precision

I believe in leveraging technology to enhance pathology services, ensuring accurate and timely diagnosis.

Accessibility & Impact

Quality pathology should be accessible to all, breaking geographical and economical barriers to improve global healthcare.

Education & Mentorship

Knowledge sharing is key to advancing the field of pathology. I am committed to training and mentoring future pathologists.

Collaboration & Leadership

Teamwork and cross-disciplinary collaboration drive meaningful change in diagnostics and patient care.

Why Work With Me

With expertise in both traditional and digital pathology, I bring a unique blend of medical knowledge and technological innovation. My experience spans diagnostics, research, project leadership, and medical education. I have worked with healthcare institutions, digital pathology platforms, and educational organizations to advance patient care and train future professionals.


By integrating cutting-edge digital pathology solutions, I help streamline workflows, improve diagnostic accuracy, and make pathology services more efficient and accessible. Whether through research, education, or industry collaboration, I am dedicated to transforming pathology for the future.
