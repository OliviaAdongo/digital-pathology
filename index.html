Empowering Pathology with Digital Precision.

Maureen Waithaka is an experienced medical doctor with specialisation in pathology. She is a passionate medical educator and a digital pathology innovator. She foresees a future where all patients in Kenya, on the continent and beyond, have access to good quality and affordable pathology resources.

An alumnus of Aga Khan University Hospital competitive and peer approved anatomic pathology residency program, she has worked both in Kenya and Namibia honing histopathology diagnostic skills.
